# Glossary

- **ASR:** automatic speech recognition.
- **DER:** diarization error rate.
- **WDER:** word-level diarization error rate or a related speaker-attributed
  word metric; define the exact implementation used.
- **WER:** word error rate.
- **Speaker diarization:** determining who spoke when.
- **Overlapping speech:** intervals containing simultaneous speakers.
- **Cross-speech:** interruptions or simultaneous speech across speakers.
- **Temporal anchor:** explicit timestamp grounding for transcript content.
- **Speaker attribution:** assigning words or segments to a speaker.
- **RAG:** retrieval-augmented generation.
- **VAD:** voice activity detection.
- **LLM correction:** constrained language-model post-processing of ASR output.
